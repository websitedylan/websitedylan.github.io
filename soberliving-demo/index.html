About Safe Steps Recovery

Safe Steps Recovery provides a structured, safe, and supportive sober living environment for individuals committed to long-term recovery. We emphasize accountability, personal responsibility, community support, and healthy daily routines.

Our Program Includes

Accountability

Daily check-ins, curfews, house meetings & peer support.

Drug Testing

Random testing to help maintain a safe, sober environment.

Mentorship

Guidance, career coaching, and personal development support.

Life Skills

Budgeting, employment support, healthy living & community resources.
