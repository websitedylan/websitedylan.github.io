About Safe Steps Recovery

Safe Steps Recovery provides a highly structured sober living environment focused on accountability, personal growth, and community support. Our program is designed to help individuals build a strong foundation for lasting recovery while developing life skills, healthy routines, and positive relationships.

Our Program

Accountability

Daily check-ins, curfews, house meetings & support system.

Drug Testing

Random testing to help ensure a safe and sober living space.

Life Skills

Support with jobs, budgeting, career coaching, & personal growth.

Community

Positive peer support & healthy sober network building.
